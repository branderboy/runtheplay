## Social Media

### Urban Podcast Intelligence Feed — Run the Play

**Concept:** Run the Play operates TikTok and Instagram accounts that publish viral moments from urban podcasts with a locked, ESPN-style lower-third stat bar. Each post adds useful show data—such as clip views, channel subscribers, Spotify chart position, social following, audience demographics, and upload frequency—so the account becomes the data layer of urban podcasting rather than another clip page.

**Audience:** The feed serves two sides:

- Brands, agencies, artists, businesses, and media buyers scouting urban podcast advertising opportunities
- Podcasters seeking visibility for their shows, audience metrics, growth, and available inventory

**Content formats:**

- Viral podcast clips with a branded stat bar
- Monthly growth rankings
- Advertising-cost breakdowns
- Underrated-show spotlights
- Head-to-head show comparisons
- Audience and demographic snapshots
- Sample campaign plans built around a specific budget or audience

**Visual identity:** Use one locked lower-third template in the same position on every video. The system uses navy and orange, bold white captions, large readable numbers, and no unnecessary visual clutter. Template recognition is the brand.

**Primary CTA:** Every post drives viewers to the Run the Play Ad Planner.

> Want your brand in this feed? Build your plan. Link in bio.

**Data sources:**

- Public YouTube video views, channel subscribers, and publishing activity
- Owner-connected YouTube Analytics for verified 28-day or monthly channel metrics and demographics
- Spotify podcast charts
- Public Instagram, TikTok, and other social follower counts
- Public RSS feeds for episode frequency and recency
- Rephonic estimates and podcast research data
- Metrics submitted and verified by podcast owners

Every displayed metric must include its source, measurement period, update date, and status: **Public**, **Verified**, or **Estimated**.

**Monetization path:** Social attention converts into Ad Planner usage, claimed-profile subscriptions, sponsored placements sold by Run the Play on its own feed, and direct inquiries sent to participating podcasts. Run the Play does not sell or broker podcast advertising inventory.

**Cadence:** Publish three to five clips daily, prioritizing strong moments from episodes released within the previous 48 hours. Add rankings, comparisons, and advertising education throughout the week.
